HomeLab

❮ ❯

Ever evolving homelab environment utilizating qemu/kvm virtualization thereby enabling a safe test bed for industry technologies, custom solutions, and experimentation. The server fundamentally focuses upon virtualization through qemu and GPU acceleration is achieve via VFIO. Thereofore, all services such as routers, storage, and build environment are virtualized. The chief choice of router is OpnSense where access to the server is gained via Wireguard. Data redunancy is ensured through nightly synchronizations betwen servers, as such all servers contain similar environments. Due to the limiations of consumer hardware, such as the lack of a BMC, a botched relay mechanism controlled by an android was emploted to ensure reboot after 10 minutes of power restoration. Power surages an mini-outages are prevented via an UPS. Additional container-based virtualization is achieved with Docker which is resposible for hosting Minecraft Servers, run AI models, provide a webbased services such as OnlyOffice and Notepad, and more. Easy access to most common configuration modifications and simple automated features are managed with an in-house cli solution.
